About ICS – TAMKOR BIOTEC

Our processing unit at Tamkor, District Jhunjhunu, Rajasthan, operates under strict quality parameters and continuous monitoring to prevent contamination, ensuring a 100% organic product. ICS produces Spirulina of the highest quality, on par with global standards, and has expanded its capacity to 47 cultivation tanks, enabling production of up to 2 tons per month. Additionally, we have invested in the latest plant and machinery at our Gujarat facility to produce Spirulina in its purest form with zero contamination.
